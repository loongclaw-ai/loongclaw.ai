The Connectivity pillar focuses on how LoongClaw communicates with the outside world—both with AI models and with users.

## In this section:

- **[LLM Providers](/docs/connectivity/providers)**: Configure connections to OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Kimi, Gemini, and more.
- **[Channels](/docs/connectivity/channels)**: Set up communication channels like Telegram, Feishu (Lark), and the CLI.
